    How to Use This Tool

    Adding Courses

    Click "Add Course" in any semester. Enter course name, credit hours, and expected/actual grade.

    Import from Template

    Click "Import" button in a semester to load official courses from your program's tentative study plan.

    Repeat Course

    If you are retaking a course, click "Repeat" on that course row. Enter your original grade so the calculator can properly replace it in your CGPA.

    Data Persistence

    Your data is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. Keep cookies/storage enabled to retain your progress.
